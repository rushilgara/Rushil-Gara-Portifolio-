About Me

My Story

Ever since I was in college, I’ve been fascinated by how technology can solve real-world problems. I started with coding, learning the basics of C and Python through online tutorials and small projects.

My interest in AI grew as I explored its potential to create smarter solutions and improve productivity. I started experimenting with AI tools, building small projects, and learning how to apply AI to real-world problems.

Along the way, I discovered the power of product building — turning ideas into working solutions. This led me to work on OpternX, an AI-powered platform that aims to help candidates access job opportunities more efficiently. Each project became a learning experience in coding, AI, and product development, shaping me into a problem-solver who loves building things from scratch.
